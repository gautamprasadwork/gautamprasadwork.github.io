Hybrid GARCH-LSTM Volatility Forecasting: A Comparative Analysis of Developed and Emerging Equity Markets

Investigating whether a hybrid econometric-deep learning architecture — combining GARCH-family models with LSTM neural networks — produces superior out-of-sample volatility forecasts vs. standalone models. Analysis spans developed markets (S&P 500, DAX) and emerging markets (NIFTY50, WIG20), providing a comparative view of model performance across market structures.
